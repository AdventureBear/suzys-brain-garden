This is an example medical story post. Share your experiences, insights, and stories from your medical career.

## What to write here:

- Patient stories (anonymized, of course)
- Medical insights and lessons learned
- Healthcare system observations
- Compassion and connection moments

## Cross-Topic Posts

Remember, your posts can span multiple topics! A medical story might also be:
- A coaching lesson → add `coaching` tag
- A personal reflection → add `personal` tag
- About burnout/wellness → add `wellness` tag

**Example tags:** `[medical, coaching, compassion, wellness]`

This way, readers interested in coaching will find your medical stories that have coaching insights!
